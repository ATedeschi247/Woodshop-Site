Learn the Basics

Expand student knowledge of cabinetmaking by learning advanced furniture manufacturing processes and the techniques used in designing and constructing kitchen cabinets, vanities, and built-ins. During the first semester, students participate in individual and group projects. The second semester focuses on the development and completion of an elaborate mass-production project.
